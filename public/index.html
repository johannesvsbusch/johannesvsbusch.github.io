Biography

I am a PhD student with Prof. Roberto Calandra at the Learning, Adaptive Systems, and Robotics (LASR) Lab at TU Dresden. My research focuses on model-based and hierarchical reinforcement learning applied to robotics.

Previously, I worked with Prof. Frank Fitzek on using machine learning in the context of communication networks and transportation systems.
